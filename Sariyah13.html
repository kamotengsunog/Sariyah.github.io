Enriquez-Gala Ancestral Mansion

The Enriquez-Gala Ancestral Mansion, also known as the Governor Natalio Enriquez Ancestral House, is a historic Art Deco landmark in Sariaya, Quezon.

it was designed by Andres Luna de San Pedro, son of the renowned artist Juan Luna. It's recognized as a National Heritage House and was a venue for high society gatherings in the pre-war era
