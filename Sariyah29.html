Dalampasigan Beach Resort

Dalampasigan Beach Resort is a Quezon province beach resort located in the municipality of Sariaya. Founded in 1990, it’s a 10-hectare private resort in Quezon province.

It has a nice pool and beach that everyone will surely enjoy whether it’s a family vacation or a company outing. They have recreational facilities and spacious ground area that can be used for bonding or team building activities.
