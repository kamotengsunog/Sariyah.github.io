Sariaya Municipal Hall

The municipality of Sariaya is a first-class municipality in Quezon province. It is known as the "Heritage Town of Quezon" and the "Art Deco Capital of Southern Luzon"

The town is known for its well-preserved ancestral homes, many of which are in the Art Deco style. The old municipal hall was previously located in the Alvarez Residence, which has since been converted into the Sariaya Museum
